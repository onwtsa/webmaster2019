The Technology Student Association's mission statement says that participation "enhances personal development, leadership, and career opportunities in STEM" by allowing members to "apply and integrate these concepts through extracirricular activities, competitions, and related programs." The Olathe Northwest chapter of TSA strives to follow these same guiding principles in the program. Olathe Northwest's TSA program is an extracirricular activity that allows students to explore various careers in different technological fields through a multitude of individual and team pojects.

Popular Events:

  • Tech Problem Solving:
    At regionals, Olathe NOrthwest asks of all participants to compete in this fun, on-site event.
  • Dragster:
    Making small cars is what most students have benn doing since they were young, so it's no suprise that making a CO2 car would appeal to the majority of participants.
  • Flight Endurance:
    On the samke toke, many students are experienced paper arline makers, and love taking their knowledge of aerodynamics into competition.
  • Technology Bowl:
    Many students like to take part in this quiz-like contest, proving how much they know about different technological subjects.
